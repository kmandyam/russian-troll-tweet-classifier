Classifying Tweets with BERT

	- troll tweets: fivethirtyeight has a dataset of 3 million russian troll tweets
	- non-troll tweets: australian election tweets + democrat vs. republican tweets + election day 2016 tweets
- Deliverables:
	- One trained BERT model
	- One live demo (ability to input a tweet, query the model, and output the result)
